1. Search for Weather Information

To check the weather for a specific location, follow these steps:

1.1. Open the Weather Website in your web browser.
1.2. Locate the search bar at the top of the homepage.
1.3. Type the name of the city or location you want to check the weather for.
1.4. Click the "Search" button.
1.5. Wait a moment while the website fetches the latest weather data for the specified location.

2. View Weather Details

Once you've searched for a location, you will be presented with the weather information for that area. Here's what you can expect:

2.1. Location Name: The name of the city or place you searched for.
2.2. Current Temperature: The current temperature at the specified location in degrees Celsius or Fahrenheit.
2.3.Weather Condition: A brief description of the current weather conditions, such as "Clear," "Partly Cloudy," "Rainy," etc.
2.4. Wind Speed: The speed of the wind in kilometers per hour (km/h) or miles per hour (mph).
2.5.Humidity: The percentage of humidity in the air.
2.6. Today's High/Low Temperature: The highest and lowest temperatures expected for the day.
2.7. Weather Icon: A graphical representation of the current weather conditions.
2.8. Weather Forecast: A brief summary of the weather forecast for the next few days.

3. Recent Searches

The Simple Weather Website keeps track of your recent searches to make it convenient for you to check the weather for your frequently visited locations. Here's how it works:

3.1. After you search for a location, the website stores the city name in your recent searches list.
3.2. To access your recent searches, scroll to the bottom of the page.
3.3. You will find a table displaying your recent cities and their respective weather conditions.
3.4. Click on a city name in the table to quickly view its weather details again.

That's it! You are now ready to use the Simple Weather Website to check weather forecasts for your desired locations and easily access your recent searches. If you have any additional questions or need assistance, don't hesitate to refer to the FAQ section or contact our support team. Enjoy using our weather forecasting platform!
